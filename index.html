My Story

In the morning of the 6th October 2001, the first child of this small family came to make their life more and more beautiful. They decided to name the baby Doha, like Surah Ad-Dhuha in the Holy Quran. The girl grew and grew up making a lot of achievements in school with love from all of her teachers. In the 10th grade, Doha won a competition named the Arab Reading Challenge and traveled to the United Arab Emirates.

She passed high school with a grade of 95.5, which is good, and entered the School of Engineering at the University of Jordan.
